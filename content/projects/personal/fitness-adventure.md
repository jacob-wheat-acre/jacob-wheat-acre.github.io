I’ve been building a small iOS app called **Fitness Adventure**.


---

## The Idea

I started building a SwiftUI app where:

- Workouts generate XP  
- XP increases level  
- Levels unlock abilities  
- Quests trigger encounters  
- Encounters reinforce effort
